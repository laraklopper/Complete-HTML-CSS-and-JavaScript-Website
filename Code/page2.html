Humans are a major and main threat to wildlife in Africa therefore, every individual has a duty to help protect the environment. Threats such as climate change also affect humans due to rising sea levels and increasing temperatures which cause wildfires which results in habitat loss and therefore threatens ecosystems which contain already endangered wildlife. It is therefore clear that there is a need to protect the environment for the benefit of both present and future generations.

In South Africa the environment is also protected by the Constitution and legislation. There are also several protective measures under several international treaties.

TOP TEN MOST ENDANGERED SPECIES IN AFRICA

  1. Black Rhino (hook-lipped rhino)
  2. African Elephant
  3. Gorilla (Eastern and Western)
  4. Saharan Cheetah
  5. African Wilddog
  6. African Penguin
  7. North African Ostrich
  8. Dama Gazelle
  9. Egyptian Toirtoise
  10. Saharan Aphinius
